Pulmonary Patients Prioritiser



How it Works


Why prioritize?

While it is ideal to treat all patients with utmost priority, treatment facilities are often outnumbered by the number of patients under care. Logically, treating the patients with higher risk first would result in least fatalities.


How to use this tool

STEP 1: Upload images by either dragging and dropping or giving individual URL's for images. This tool currently works for 8 images only.

STEP 2: Scroll to A vs B chart. This chart shows a table of comparison of indiviual patients with other patients

STEP 3: Scroll to Images by Rank. Based on the A vs B chart, this is a sorted list of patients with the top entry showing the most serious patient.
